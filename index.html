Week 6 — mid-point assessment
Your goal: confirm direction, identify gaps early, produce an action plan for anything flagged. The delta between now and week 12 is what the final decision is based on.
Week 6
Assessment checklist
  • Run Barley Prompt A — technical picture
  • Run Barley Prompt B — goal progress
  • Upload mentor prompt file to Claude
  • Complete Claude interview — all 5 questions
  • Share to Barley Slack thread (48h before meeting)
  • Attend assessment meeting — Mentor + L&D/PBP + intern
  • Review goals — on track / needs attention / off track
  • Agree action plan for anything flagged
What you're evaluating
Technical output — tickets shipped, complexity, PR quality
Independence — how much help did they need?
AI tool usage — active and reflective, or copy-paste?
Problem-solving — explores solutions or relies on guidance?
Gaps — where do you still see the ceiling?

Week 12 — final assessment and resolution
Your goal: a clear, evidence-based recommendation. Offer, extension, or no offer — communicated in person on the same day. No vague outcomes, no delays.
Week 12
Final assessment
  • Run Barley Prompts A, B, C
  • Upload mentor prompt file to Claude
  • Complete Claude interview — compare to mid-point
  • Share to Barley Slack thread (48h before meeting)
  • Attend final meeting — Mentor + L&D/PBP + intern
  • Review goals completion + 12-week trajectory
Resolution — same day
  • Agree recommendation with L&D/PBP in advance
  • Communicate in person — offer / extension / no offer
  • Not by email. Not vaguely. That day.
The growth delta — how much the intern improved from week 6 to week 12 — carries as much weight as where they ended up.
